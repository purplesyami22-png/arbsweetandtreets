All orders must be confirmed before processing,

Full Payment or a down payment may be required before order preparation.
Payments are non-refundable once production has started.

3. Delivery

Delivery time depends on the customer's location and order availability.
Delays may occur due to weather conditions or unexpected situations.

4. Cancellation Policy

Orders may only be cancelled if preparation has not yet started.
